Idea 1 - Provenance Collage

  • Cropped parts of different stolen, illegal, or contested museum artifacts that depict some (human?) figure load in. Ideally, the cropped images would be composed into a full body (head, torso, 2 arms, 2 legs, but this might have to be adjusted based on the content available) to emphasize the fragmentation and displacement of artifacts from its place of origin.
  • Images of artifacts taken from various museums galleries such as https://www.britishmuseum.org/about-us/british-museum-story/contested-objects-collection or https://www.metmuseum.org/art/collection

Idea 2 - Painting Gallery

  • Explorer for different paintings. The user can select a color, and a painting that includes that color will load in through showing different colors in the painting's palette before the image loads. Then, the image is scaled to a relative pixel size based on its real life dimensions. User can repeat this then scroll through the gallery of images.
  • Images of paintings taken from either National Gallery or the Met, which both have datasets/API https://github.com/NationalGalleryOfArt/opendata/tree/main/data or https://metmuseum.github.io/
  • similar concept to https://artsandculture.google.com/color?col=GREEN but simpler
